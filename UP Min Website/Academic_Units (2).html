Department of Mathematics, Physics & Computer Science

The DMPCS is the largest department of the College of Science and Mathematics, UP Mindanao. It offers two of the four undergraduate degree programs in the College. Around fifty percent (50%) of the College's faculty members and students belongs to the department.

Its degree programs are the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) and the Bachelor of Science in Applied Mathematics (BSAM).
